## Device configuration
Devices section shows which devices are currently configured, clicking on a specific device will open it's own dashboard 

An endpoint has 3 status :

Green: the device is connected and online 

Red: The device is connected but offline 

Grey: Device is initalized, but needs pre-configuration or to be powered on
